    PyData Dublin at 404

    Next Saturday, PyData Dublin will be heading to the Royal Hospital, Kilmainham for 404, an event which describes itself as:

    The Ultimate Tech Meetup

    It’s an all day affair with a variety of speakers and installations from different tech communities. The agenda itself can be found here. Personally, I’m really looking forward to just being at the same venue as 40+ different Dublin tech meetup organizers! I’m also excited to see Katharine Jarmul, the person who first got me interested in PyData in the first place.

    All in all, it looks set to be a fun and simulating day out for tech enthusiasts of all levels. It has even been featured as a part of RTE’s Tech News! Needless to say, we’re definitely thrilled to have a chance to be there as PyData Dublin. If you’d like to join us, tickets can be purchased here and are only €22.50 when you use the 10% discount code: PYDATA-DUB
